About

Hi, I'm Alanna! I'm a Toronto-based web developer and graduate of HackerYou. Web development has redefined my passion for communications and strategic content creation. Through HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I've learned the power of web inclusivity and I'm eager to work with tech teams that are bringing people together, on and offline.

Prior to becoming a web developer, I worked in the non-profit sector in varying communications and logistics roles. My career highlights include writing a speech that was delivered to President Barack Obama in 2017, and my role as the manager of the volunteer program for Toronto's first-ever Pride Month in 2016. I'm a goal-oriented, positive team player, and all around problem-solver. I'd love to connect and learn how we can work together.
